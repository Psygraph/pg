Graph

The Graph tool displays selected events or the signals from those events as a function of time (you select events with the List tool). Tap one on the graph to select it, and then pinch, zoom, or triple click to alter the graph axes. Different signals can be displayed, such as the amount of movement over time or your mindfulness when counting breaths.

The settings let you control which signals to display:

  • Choose signals such as accelerometer or bluetooth data, stopwatch time, or correct/incorrect breath count.
  • Specify the width of the bars in the bar chart (i.e. the aggregation interval).
