Graph

The Graph tool displays several signals as a function of time. You can use it to drag and zoom the display of selected events (events can be selected or unselected with the List tool). Triple-clicks zoom the graph to fit the data. By changing the type of signal, you can view the amount of movement over time or breath-counting accuracy.

The settings control display of selected events:

  • Graph signals such as accelerometer, time measured by the stopwatch and timer, or correct/incorrect breath count.
  • Specify the width of the bars in the bar chart.
