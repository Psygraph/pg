Welcome to Psygraph.

Psygraph keeps track of your meditation, breathing, and mindfulness through the use of tools such as a timer, counter, periodic gong, and note-taker. It allows you to visualize your behavior over time, and optionally publish that data to a WordPress site.

For more information or to create an account, please visit Psygraph.
