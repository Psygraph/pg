Stopwatch

The Stopwatch tool records your meditation sessions or other activities. The reset button (on the left) and start/stop button (on the right) allow you to reset, start, and stop the clock.

The settings let you:

  • Collect various data along with the event (from your phone or a Bluetooth device).
  • Specify that location data should be gathered continuously.
  • Specify the data sampling rate.
  • Show or hide the real-time graph.
