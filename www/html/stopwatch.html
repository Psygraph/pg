Stopwatch

The Stopwatch tool records your meditation sessions or other activities. The reset button (on the left) and start/stop button (on the right) allow you to reset, start, and stop the clock.

The settings let you:

  • Collect various device data during the event (acceleration, orientation, location, or random data).
  • Collect Bluetooth data during the event (from heart rate meters or other devices).
  • Access settings for the local device or bluetooth device.
  • Show or hide the real-time graph.
