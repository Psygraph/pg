Counter

The Counter tool measures mindfulness via breath counting. Every time you breathe, press the black button: this will draw an enso (or circle) on the screen. When you reach 10 breaths, press the white button (the enso turns green when it is time to do this). During meditation, triple-click on the page to display a screen with full-screen buttons. After meditation, the percentage of correct responses can be plotted on the graph.

The settings enable you to:

  • Change the count target.
  • Set the feedback for a correct/incorrect response.
  • Show/hide the enso.
  • Change the motion (shake) sensitivity.
  • Change the behvaior when a shake occurs.
