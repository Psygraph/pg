Counter

The Counter tool measures mindfulness via breath counting. Every time you breathe, press the black button: this will slowly draw an enso (or circle) on the screen. After counting 10 breaths, press the white button (the enso turns green when it is time to do this). Triple-click on the page to display full-screen buttons for use during meditation.

The settings enable you to:

  • Change the count target.
  • Set the feedback for a correct/incorrect response.
  • Change the motion (shake) sensitivity.
  • Change the behvaior when a shake occurs.
