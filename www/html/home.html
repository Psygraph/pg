Home

The Home page displays a summary of your data over the last several days or weeks. Data is displayed that lets you track both summary statistics and time spent in a category. To visit Psygraph.com, click on the PSI symbol.

The settings let you specifiy which signals are tracked and the length of history to show. For each page, the following data is displayed:

  • Home: The length of time the app was open.
  • Stopwatch: The length of time logged.
  • Counter: The ratio of correct/incorrect counts.
  • Timer: The ratio of mindful vs. non-mindful alarms.
  • Note: Whether a note was made or not.
