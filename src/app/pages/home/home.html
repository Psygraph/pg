Info

The Home page displays a summary of your data over the last several days or weeks, such as the cumulative amount of time spent time spent in a particular category.

The settings let you specify which signals are tracked and the length of history to show. For each category, the following data can be displayed:

  • Home: The length of time the app was open.
  • Stopwatch: The length of time logged.
  • Counter: The total count.
  • Counter: correct: The ratio of correct to incorrect counts.
  • Timer: The length of time a timer was active.
  • Timer: mindful: The ratio of mindful vs. non-mindful alarms.
  • Note: The number of notes made.
