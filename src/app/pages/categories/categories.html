Info

All events (such as stopwatch start/stop times) are assigned to a category. Four categories are present by default: Uncategorized, Meditate, Exercise, and Think.

The categories page lets you add, remove, and configure these categories. Every category has settings that govern its behavior, such as a particular sound, background color, and set of strings that are displayed by the timer alarms. If you are uploading data to a WordPress server, that data is tagged with its associated category.

The current category can be changed on the settings tab of any page.
