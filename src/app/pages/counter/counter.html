Info

The Counter tool measures mindfulness via breath counting. You might use it to count how many breaths you can take without a thought occurring, or to count your breaths until you reach a particular target. In either case, press the black (start) button every time you breathe, and press the white (reset) button when a thought occurs (or after counting a certain number of breaths).

The settings enable you to:

  • Set the count target.
  • Set the feedback for a correct/incorrect response.
  • Change the motion (shake) sensitivity.
  • Change the behvaior when a shake occurs.
